Columns in cohortData.csv 1: SampleID (matches SampleID in segmentation mask)2: cellLabelInImage3: cellSize4-6, 11, 45-46: Elemental channels or background channels (C, Na, Si, Background, Au, Ta)7: nuclear channel (HH3)8-9, 12-44: Phenotypic markers47: Tissue source (gran_lung, gran_pleura, gran_endo, gran_LN, gran_sarcoid)48: Patient ID (two SampleID values per patient)49: major cell lineage (immune, epithelial, endothelial, fibroblast)50: FlowSOM cluster51: specific cell lineage (granulocyte, lymphocyte, myeloid, imm_other, fibroblast, epithelial, endothelial)
